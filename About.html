About Us


I designed this C++ website guide as a totally free tool intended to aid you in your C++ learning process. As a beginner you might little to no prior knowledge and/or experience on C++, whatever the case maybe, fear not, the tutorials I designed on this site will walk you through each and every aspect of this programming language that you’ll need to know in order to design and compile a C++ program. It is not a magic tool that will make you an expert on the subject matter in one day, but it is a powerful and tested tool which if used with patience, will get you where you need to be.

The good thing about this webpage is it's completely free. The whole content is completely free with no subscription needed to do. C++ Programming language.com is totally, 100% free, no hidden fees, no taxes, and no license and documentation charges. So, the obvious question is, “what’s in it for us?”. Two things: We love to teach, and we love to talk about programming. Furthermore, our readers are creative, inventive, and very intelligent -- sometimes they teach us stuff in return! So we learn while we teach you, and that makes us better in our careers or hobbies. We’re just trying to make the world a better place.
